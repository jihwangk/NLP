Svépravice

Svépravice is a village and municipality ("obec") in Pelhřimov District in the Vysočina Region of the Czech Republic.
The municipality covers an area of , and has a population of 116 (as at 3 July 2006).
Svépravice lies approximately north of Pelhřimov, north-west of Jihlava, and south-east of Prague.

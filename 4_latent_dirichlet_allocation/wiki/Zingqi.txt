Zingqi

Zingqi is a village in the south-east of the Tibet Autonomous Region of China with a latitude (DMS) of 29° 30' 0N and a longitude of 92° 25' 0E . It lies at an altitude of 4821 metres (15,820 feet).
The village is located some 12.5 miles east of Rutog.

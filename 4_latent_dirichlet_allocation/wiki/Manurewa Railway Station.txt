Manurewa Railway Station

Manurewa Railway Station is on the Southern Line of the Auckland railway network. The station has a side platform layout connected by a pedestrian footbridge. Access is from both Selwyn Road and SouthMall Shopping Centre.
The station has a large park-and-ride facility and interchange with many local bus services.

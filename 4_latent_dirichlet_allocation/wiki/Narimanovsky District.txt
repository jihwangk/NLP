Narimanovsky District

Narimanovsky District () is an administrative and municipal district (raion), one of the eleven in Astrakhan Oblast, Russia. It is located in the southwest of the oblast. The area of the district is . Its administrative center is the town of Narimanov. Population: 44,077 (2002 Census); The population of Narimanov accounts for 25.3% of the district's total population.

Pełczyska, Łódź Voivodeship

Pełczyska is a village in the administrative district of Gmina Wartkowice, within Poddębice County, Łódź Voivodeship, in central Poland. It lies approximately north-east of Wartkowice, north-east of Poddębice, and north-west of the regional capital Łódź.

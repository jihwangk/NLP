Niagara District Secondary School

Niagara District Secondary School (NDSS) was a public secondary school located in Niagara-on-the-Lake, Ontario, Canada. It featured a specialized arts program focusing on the dramatic arts, dance, visual arts, and music. It offered the possibility of earning a specialized DNA arts certificate upon graduation.

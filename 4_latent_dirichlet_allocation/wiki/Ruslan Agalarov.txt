Ruslan Agalarov

Ruslan Agabekovich Agalarov (; born 21 February 1974 in Makhachkala) is a retired Russian (Dagestani) football midfielder.
He has 1 cap for the national team. His younger brother Kamil Agalarov is also a professional footballer.
He is the current head coach of the FC Anzhi Makhachkala youth team.

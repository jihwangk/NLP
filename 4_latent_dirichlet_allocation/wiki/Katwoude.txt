Katwoude

Katwoude is a village in the Dutch province of North Holland. It is a part of the municipality of Waterland, and lies on the coast of the IJsselmeer, about 2Â km north of Monnickendam.
Katwoude was a separate municipality between 1817 and 1991, when it became part of Waterland.
The statistical area "Katwoude", which covers the area of the former municipality, has a population of around 230.
